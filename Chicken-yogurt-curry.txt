Ingredients:
 - 1 pkg chicken thighs
 - 1 cup yogurt
 - 2 ripe tomatoes, chopped
 - 1 medium onion, chopped fine
 - lots of spices
 - ghee, as required

Directions:
 1. fry the onion on low heat in the ghee, until golden
 2. add spices and continue frying until aromatic
 3. add yogurt and tomatoes, simmer until very thick
 4. add chicken, turn heat to low and simmer 1 hour.
 5. enjoy
